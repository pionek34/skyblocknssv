SYSTEMY W UTILS5.SK:
====================

1. **PET SYSTEM (kompleksowy):**
   - Pet menu GUI (/pet)
   - Pet spawning (display entities: item + text)
   - Pet leveling (exp, level 1-100, rarity 1-6)
   - Pet stats (Exp multiplier, Gems multiplier)
   - Pet formatting (kolory według rarity, lore, progress bar)
   - Pet tracking (follow gracza co 4 ticki)
   - Pet particles (crit particles)
   - Pet respawn po śmierci/world change
   - Pet despawn przy quit

2. **ADMIN PET SYSTEM:**
   - /adminpet create/delete/icon/exp/gems/give/egg/list
   - Pet creation/deletion
   - Pet stats configuration
   - Pet giving

3. **MAGIC EGG SYSTEM:**
   - Pet Egg item
   - Hatching animation (spawn, sounds, particles)
   - Random pet generation
   - Ender dragon animation effect

4. **PORTAL MANAGEMENT:**
   - Portal breaking protection
   - Remove portal GUI (confirm/cancel)
   - Only island owner can remove
